About Us

EventsNow is an event management company which started in 2007.Established with a vision to bring something fresh to the stage we aim to create timeless events that would be treasured for a lifetime.Whatever be the occasion of importance, a conference or a wedding, an anniversary or an entertainment show, Road show or Advertising, we are just so ready for it.

To top it all, we are endowed with the competence to handle things ‘from start to the finish’ as we possess a deep understanding of every nuance attached to a particular event. What’s more? This will be done well within your means.

So, come home to Ideal, give us your idea of an occasion, budget and timeline; rest assured you’ll have a flawlessly fine event unfolding beyond your expectations.

‘EventsNow’ is swarmed with thorough, dynamic and enthusiastic professionals fully devoted to achieve success in every effort they undertake. With an eye for minutest detail, they induce striking opulence to the events by blending creativity with sensible flair. Carrying an attitude that demands no less than perfection, they bring about uncompromising standards of quality to deliver our clients with the privilege of ‘The Mind at Peace’.
